Chef Mario

Meet Chef Mario, our head chef with 20 years of experience in Italian cuisine. La Trattoria Italiana is a family-owned restaurant nestled in the heart of Florence, serving authentic Italian dishes crafted from time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Since 1985, our passion has been to bring the true flavors of Italy to your table with fresh, locally sourced ingredients and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Our story began with Nonna Rosa's secret recipes, blending simplicity and tradition in every bite. We pride ourselves on creating a cozy dining experience where every guest feels like part of the family. From our classic pasta to our wood-fired pizzas and homemade desserts, each dish is a celebration of Italy's rich culinary heritage. Join us for a meal and experience the heart and soul of Italian cuisine — because here, food is more than just nourishment; it's a way to connect, celebrate, and create memories.
